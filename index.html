About Youthcosum Kaduna

The Youth Collaboration on Summit (youthcosum) is a forum where young professionals in various fields including; Fin-tech, Cyber-security, Renewable-Energy,Machine Learning, Fashion, Music, Photography, Social Media Advertising, Youth Policy, Legal Services,Agribusiness, Health/Wellness, Sports, Internet-of- Things, Retail/wholesale, etcetera, converge to engage in cooperative interactions at all levels to increase the socio-economic well-being of the youths....
